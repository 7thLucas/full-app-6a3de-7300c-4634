# Cafe Shop Landing Page — Product Overview

## What It Is
A clean, focused two-section landing page for a cafe shop. Designed to give cafes an instant, beautiful web presence that showcases their atmosphere and highlights their signature offerings — all within a single, scrollable page.

## Users
- **Primary**: Prospective customers discovering the cafe online (via search, social media, or word of mouth)
- **Secondary**: The cafe owner who wants to manage their brand story and menu online without a developer

## Positioning
A minimal yet premium landing page — warm, inviting, and conversion-focused. Not a full e-commerce platform or reservation system; just the right two sections to turn a curious visitor into a walk-in customer.

## Two Sections (Product Scope)
1. **Hero Section** — Full-width opening with the cafe name, a tagline that sets the mood, a striking background image, and a clear call-to-action button directing visitors to explore the menu.
2. **Menu Section** — A curated grid of signature menu items (with name, price, and description) paired with a brief intro that communicates the cafe's personality and values.

## Brand & Tone
- Warm, cozy, artisanal
- Calm and welcoming — like the cafe itself
- Not cluttered; every element earns its space
- Editorial and premium, not generic or corporate

## Brand Colors
- **Primary**: Rich coffee brown — `#6F4E37`
- **Secondary**: Warm cream — `#F5E6D0`
- **Accent**: Golden amber — `#C8860A`

## Typography
- Elegant serif headlines (Georgia)
- Clean, readable body text

## App Name
My Cafe *(placeholder — updated once the owner names their cafe)*

## Tagline
"Where every cup tells a story"

## Strategic Principles
- **Less is more**: Two sections done brilliantly beat ten sections done generically
- **Conversion-first**: Every element exists to move the visitor one step closer to visiting the cafe
- **Owner-editable**: Menu items, name, tagline, and colors are all configurable — no developer needed for updates
- **Fast and focused**: No backend, no cart, no reservations in the MVP; just a beautiful, effective page

## Scope (MVP)
Two sections only — Hero + Menu/About. No e-commerce, no booking, no login. A polished, live page the owner can be proud of on day one.
